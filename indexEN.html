About Us

MatexPol started operating in 2012, our specialty is road transport using our own truck fleet. Our services cover all directions and our truck can be found both nationally and throughout Eastern and Western Europe.

We have a qualified team of employees who are ready to provide services of the highest quality standard. The competent staff will provide the most suited and effective logistics solutions and a convenient way to send goods safely to your final destination.

Quick

A modern fleet and experienced drivers allow us to deliver goods quickly and professionally. Our specialists will estimate the delivery time of each load

Safely

Our modern and reliable fleet with certifcate EURO 6, will allow your product to reach its destination safely

Professionally

We are able to organize not only the transport of goods, but also all necessary activities related to its transport, such as customs clearance, ferry crossings and storage.
